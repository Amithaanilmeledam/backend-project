How It Works

Our platform connects concerned citizens with local authorities to address stray dog issues effectively.

Report the Case

Fill out the form with location, urgency, and details about the stray dogs.

Authority Notified

Your report is sent to the relevant panchayat or municipality.

Action Taken

Local authorities will assess and take appropriate action.

Reporting Guidelines

  • Provide accurate location information for quick response
  • Include photos if possible to help authorities assess the situation
  • Select the appropriate urgency level honestly
  • Keep your contact information updated for follow-up communication
